Features

Classic Deathmatch

Deathmatch, Team DM, Capture the Flag, Instagib and Rocket Arena gameplay modes.

Legendary Maps

High quality remakes of id Software's legendary Quake series deathmatch levels, plus many originals.

Modern Rendering

Realtime lighting with soft shadowmapping, bump mapping with parallax occlusion, and volumetric fog.

Level Editing

Dramatically improved artwork pipeline with in-game material editor and TrenchBroom support.

Cross Platform

Runs on macOS, Linux, BSD and Windows. Play with friends on any platform.

Free & Open Source

100% free to download, play and modify. Licensed under GPL v2.
